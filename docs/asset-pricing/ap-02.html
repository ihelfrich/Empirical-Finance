AP-02: CAPM + SDF Foundations

Build the CAPM Twice and See Why It Must Agree

This module is built to make CAPM feel inevitable: first through mean-variance logic, then through the stochastic discount factor. We do every derivation in full and pin each step to its economic meaning.

Core Equation

E[R_i] - R_f = beta_i (E[R_M] - R_f)

We derive this twice and explain where each term comes from.

SDF Bridge

E[m R_i] = 1

One pricing equation unifies equilibrium and no-arbitrage logic.
