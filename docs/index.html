Qualifier Map

The journey is structured as a staged launch: foundations, drills, and full simulations. Every stage is anchored to real qualifier questions and known exam patterns.

Stage 1: Build the Engine

Review core theorems, notation discipline, and working algebra under pressure.

Stage 2: Systematic Drills

Problem sets become method rehearsals. Each solution is annotated with pitfall alerts.

Stage 3: Qualifier Simulations

Timed, end-to-end exam practice with full debrief and alternate strategies.

Pedagogical Method

The method is simple: motivation first, derivation in full, and then clean interpretation. I slow down at the points where students usually get stuck.

Motivation First

Every topic begins with a problem and a reason to care about the result.

Step-by-Step Proofs

No skipped algebra. Every line is written so you can reproduce it under exam pressure.

Exam-Style Narration

Solutions read like a calm oral defense: what to do, why it works, and how to check it.

Reflection + Variants

Each core problem branches into extensions that force you to transfer the idea.
